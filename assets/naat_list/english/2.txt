                                Anwaar-E-Muhammed

Dil Mein Hai Basi Mere Tanveer Muhammed Ki
Aankhon Ne Hai Ab Leli Tasveer Muhammed Ki

Noor Aisa Mila Mujhko Noori Ke Tassadukh Se 
Harr Zarre Mein Milti Hai Tanveer Muhammed Ki

Ab Ankh Meri Aise Kotha Na Samaj Zaahid 
Jo Dekh Na Sakti Hai Tasveer Muhammed Ki

Murshid Ne Mere Aise Parde Ko Uthayaa Hai
Khud Peer Mein Dekhi Hai Tasveer Muhammed Ki

Irshaad Ki Sohbath Mein Hum Tujhse Hai Kya Sunte
Noori Teri Baaton Mein Taqreer Muhammed Ki

Kya Tujh Ko Pukaroo'n Mai Kya Naam Tera Lu Mai
Seekhi Teri Sohbath Mein Taqreer Muhammed Ki

Vo Ek Nazar Mein Hai Aur Ek Mein Sab Kuch Hai
Ein Aalaam-O-Aa'n Aalaam Tanveer Muhammed Ki

Aamir Na Mufassir Tha Aalim Tha Na Aarif Tha
Noori Se Padhi Usne Tafseer Muhammed Ki
