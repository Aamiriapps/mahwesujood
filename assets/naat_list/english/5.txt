                                    Madeene Waale

Jaan Tum Par Karu'n 
Qurbaa'n Madeene Waale

Tumse Mujh Ko Mila 
Rehmaan Madeene Waale

Kaun Jaane Tumhe Jaane 
Toh Khudaa Hi Jaane

Tumsa Koi Nahi'n 
Insaan Madeene Waale

Teri Haar Baat Pe Dil 
Sadqe To Qurbaan Ye Jaan

Tere Bin Sab Rahe 
Anjaan Madeene Waale

Dars-E-Irfaan Tere 
Dar Se Hai Haasil Hota 

Tere Dam Se Hai Ye 
Faizaan Madeene Waale

Tere Dam Se Hai Basa 
A'alam-E-Imkaan Saara

Saach Hai Laulaq Teri 
Shaan Madeene Waale

Ye Yaqee'n Aap Ke 
Sadqe Mein Mila A'alam Ko

Shak Gaya Reh Gaya 
E'khaan Madeene Waale

Parda Kar Ke Ye Dikhaya 
Ke Mai Parde Mein Bhi Hoon

Phir Bhi Zaahir Hai Tu 
Har Aan Madeene Waale

Tere Jalwe Ki Haqeeqat 
Koi Tujhse Puche

Tujhse Zaahir Huwa 
Rehmaan Madeene Waale

Hai Butun-E-Ahadi Jo 
Hai Zahoor Ahmed Ka 

Mann-Ra-Aani Mein Hai 
Pehchaan Madeene Waale

Hazrath-E-Haqq Ko Vo 
Dekhega Jo Tujh Ko Dekhe

Gar Khuda De Tera 
Irfaan Madeene Waale

Kya Likhoon Naat Teri 
Khaama Mera Ruk Ta Hai

Daftaar-E-Naat Hai 
Quraan Madeene Waale

Usko Noori Mein Toh Aur Tujh
Mein Khudaa Milta Hai 

Yahin Aamir Ka Hai 
Irfaan Madeene Waale
