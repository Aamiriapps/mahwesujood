                             TAJDAR-E-MADEENA

Hamari Taraf Bhi Madeene Ke Malik
Khudara Zaraa Barrmala Dekhna

Hamari Tabahi Ti Aasma'n Ab
Nabi Ji Hamare Zaraa Dekhna

Tabeeban-E-A'alam Ye Kya Jante Hain
Mera Haal-E-Dil Mustafa Dekhna

Agar Tum Na Dekho To Phir Kaun Dekhe
Baraa-E-Ali Murtaza Dekhna

Dar-E-Mustafa Ke Talashi Se Keh Du'n
Dar-E-Haidari Ka Pata Dekhna

Hamein To Nabi Ko Sada Dekhna Hai
Nahi'n Kuch Jazaa, Sazaa Dekhna

Munazza Ka Jalwa Mushabba Ki Surat
Aur Aaina-E-Mustafa Dekhna

Fatahaath Inki Ghulami Mein Aaye
Sagaan-E-Dar-E-Mustafa Dekhne
