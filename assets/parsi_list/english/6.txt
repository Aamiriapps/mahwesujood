                              SYED-UL-BASHAAR

Mareez-E-Tu Am Kun, Dawa Ya Mohammed
Bajuz Tu Na Daaram Shafa Ya Mohammed

Panah-E-Do Aalam Tuyi, Aajizaa’n Raa
Karam Kun Bara-e-Khudaa Ya Mohammed

Nadaram Suku’n Dardilam Heechgona
Sukun-E-Dil-O-Jaan-E-Maa Ya Mohammed

Zabaa’n Khushk Gashtha, Dil Azz Seena Raftha
Nadaram Bajuz Ek Sadaa Ya Mohammed

Gada-E-Pareshaa’n Baa Dargaah-E-Zeeshaa’n
Ba-Khawanad Tera Tha-Kuja Ya Mohammed

Gunah Karda Bae-Hadd Pareshan Gashtham
Tuyi Aasiyaa’n Raa Dawa Ya Mohammed

Ba-Soyam Nazar Kun, Khudaa Raa Karam Kun
Wagar Na Ba-Meerad Gadaa Ya Mohammed

Cha-Goyam Tera Kesthi Darr-Haqeeqath
Sana-E-Toh Goyad Khuda Ya Mohammed

Ba-Gufthi Ke “Kunthu Nabiyan” Zae-Awwal
Tuyi Ibtada Inteha Ya Mohammed

Tuyi Noor-E-Emaa’n Tuyi Jaan-E-Jaanaa’n
Tuyi Qura’n-E-Maa, Deen-E-Maa Ya Mohammed

Khudaa Jalwa Karda Ba-Shaan-E-Tu Shaha
Tuyi Ayat-E-“Ainama” Ya Mohammed

Hama Khalq Joyad Raza-E-Ilahi
Raza-E-Toh Joyad Khuda Ya Mohammed

Zae-Faizaan-E-Noori Bago Aamira Toh
Shab-O-Roz “Aalle Alaa” Ya Mohammed
