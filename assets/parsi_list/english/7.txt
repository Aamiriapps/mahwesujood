                              SHAHEENSHAH-E-WILAYAT

Reham-Kun Behar-E-Khudaa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir
Kun Nazar Suu-E-Gadaa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Gharq-Shud Darr Behar-E-Aasiyan Kashti-E-Bechargaa’n
Shou-Khudara Na Khudaa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Khaqsaram Aajizam Haal-E-Pareshanam Babee’n
Ba-Shunwa Ek Nawaa’n Ek Sada Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Mann Zaleel-O-Khawar Gashtha, Aamadam Barr-Aastaa’n
Dar-Kusha Behar-E-Khudaa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Tu Ghayas-Ul-Aalameeni, Mustaghas-ul-aalameeni
Ras ba-far yaad-e-gada Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Mushkilam Aasaa’n Shud Azz Naam-E-Paakath Shah-E-Mann
Jaan-E-Mann Barr Tu Fidaa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Tu Muhiyudeen Gashthi Deen Raa Zindaa Kuni
Mann Fida Barr Aei’n Adaa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Deen-E-Maa Imaan-E-Maa-O-Jaan-E-Maa Janan-E-Maa
Har-Chae Goyad Hasth Rawaa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Qaul-e-“Qadami Haaza” Maqbool Tazd-E-Auliya
Munkirash Ya Badd Sazaa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Auliya Hasthnad Zair-E-Paa-E-Pa-Kath Bil-Yaqeen
Tu Shahinshah-E-Wilaa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Mazhar-E-Noor-E-Nabi Hasti Tu Aye Ghaus-Ul-Wara
Zill-E-Noor-E-Mustafa Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Dast-E-Tu Dast-E-Mohammed Dast-E-Ahmad Dast-E-Khalq
Dast-E-Haqq Dast-E-Shuma Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir

Dast-E-Noori Dast-E-Toh-O-Dast-E-Nabi
Goyad Aamir Dayima Ya Ghouse Azam Dastagir
