                      HUSN-E-YAGANAA

Man Cheez-E-Na Bainam, Bajuz Aa’n Husn-E-Yagana
Ko Taer Shud Sat Vo Dil-E-Ishaaq Nashaana

Tu Shahid-O-Mash-Hud Dar-E-Dair-O-Haram Khud
Man Aajiz-O-Madum Beher Ellaa Bahana

Tu Tan Shudi Tu Dil Shudi Tu Jaa’n Shudi Do Aalam
Har Chay Shudaa Az-Tu Shuda Dar Kaun-O-Makaana

Tu Sakeenay Aalam Shudi Aalam Bataou Saabit
Har Jaa Tuyi Zaahir Shudi Har Aan-O-Zamana

Awwal Tuyi Aakhir Tuyi Zaahir Tuyi Baatin
Yaani Ke Tuyi Aamadi Har Jaa Ba Zamama

Mann Dair-O-Ha'ram Deedam Vo Cheez-E-Nami Yabam
Toh Dair-O-Ha'ram Ghasti Vo To Maal-O-Khazana

Dar Dil Shudaaayi Saakin Vo Jaye Toh Dilam Shud
Dil Khana-E-Toh Ghasta Vo Toh Saahib-E-Khanaa

Azhar Dil-E-Aalam Tera Har Aan Bajoyam
Shayad Ke Tu Razi Shuyi Azman Ba Bahana

Aalam Ra Chu’n Bainam Hama Mautaaj-E-Toh Bainam
Tu Maalik-E-Aalam Shudi Tu Shah-E-Zamana

Tu Qadir-E-Muthlaaq Shudi Tu Khaaliq-E-Aalam
Maujood Darr-E-Kaun-O-Makaa’n Tu Shudi Maana

Tu Zaahir-O-Baatin Shudi Tu Awwal-O-Aakhir
Har Su Shudi Har Jaa Shudi-O-Khana Bakhana

Har Aa’n Tera Bainam Ke Tuyi Aain-E-Do Aalam
Zaahir Tuyi Baatin Tuyi Qaayam Tuyi Ma-Na

Zinda Tuyi Dana Tuyi Khwahaa’n -O-Tawana
Shunwa Tuyi Baina Tuyi Goya Tuyi Maana

Harsu Tuyi Jalwa Kun-O-Harsu Tuyi Tu Be Aayi
Chun Parda Ba Khwahi Shuyi Dar Shakle Rawana

Chun Khwahi Biyaii Dar-E-Aalam Bulbasay
Chun Khwahi Kuni Tarke Libaash Ba Bahana

Zaahir Shudi Barrman Bulbulasay Tera Deedam
Namash Shuda Noori Ke Shud Yakta-E-Zamaana

Aamira Tu Khuda Bain-O-Khuda Daa’n Azo Ghasti
Aa’n Shaikh Ke’h Shud Rehbar-E-Peeran-E-Zamaana
