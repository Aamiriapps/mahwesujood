                      PAS-E-PARDA-E-RAAZ

Tu Harr Dam Yaad Me Aayi, 
Tera Harr Baar Me Bainam

Tamasha-E-Ajab Bainam, 
Ajab Darbar Me Bainam

Baai’n Aalam Tuyi Zaahir, 
Baa’n A'alam Tyui Baatin

Toh Ru-Poshi Ya Bemayi,
Magar Ruqsar Me Bainam

Na Bainad Khalq Jarm-E-Mann, 
Magar Badnaam Me Saa-Zad

Tu Beeni Har Damm-O-Laykin, 
Tera Sattar Me Bainam

Agarche Neesth Dar Aalam, 
Misal-E-Mann Gunah-Gaa-Ray

Miyan-E-Dar Raheema’n Khawaish-Ra, 
Har Bar Me Bainam

Kisay Ko Dil Buto Faroqat, 
Jaan Qurban-E-Ouu Saazam

Aqar Au Raahmi Bainam, 
Bago-Yam Yaar Me Bainam

Gahe Ba’r-Paa-E-Oo Sar Karda,
Goyam Tu Nayi Wallah

Kamal-E-Haqq Buto Yaa-Bam, 
Jamal-E-Yaar Me Bainam

Azo Pursam Ke Tu Bashri,
Bagoya Tu Malak Hasti

Tu Aakhir Keesti Darr 
Tu Ajab Anwaar Me Bainam

Bago-Yad-Mann Nayam Cheez-E-Bajuz, 
Aa’n Jaan-E-Jaana Namm

Agar Cheez-E-Hami Bainam, 
Dar-O-Dildar Me Bainam

Qadam Boseeda Purseedam 
Ke Naamath Cheest Aye Aaqa

Ba-Gufth Aa’n Nooryam Har Dam, 
Bakhud Aa’n Yaar Me Bainam

Ba-Guftham Aye Shah-E-Waala, 
Nazar Barman Bakun La’h

Ke Aamir Ra-Hamesha 
Khasth-O-Nachar Me Bainam
