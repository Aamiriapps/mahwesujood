                   YAAR-O-AGHYAAR

Sanama Tu Jaan-E-Jahaa'n Shudi,
Tu Makee’n Shudi Tu Makaa’n Shudi

Zay-Zamee’n Tu Tabaa Sama Shudi 
Gahey Aa’n Shudi Gahe Aa’n Shudi

Toh Ba-Hhar Makaa’n Ba-Wajood-e-Khud,
To Ba Har Zamaa’n Ba-Zahur-E- Khud

Gahe Parda Kardi Zear-E-Ghair-E-Khud,
Gahe Aine Jaan-E-Jahaa’n Shudi

Gahe Jalwa-E-Tu Qamar Bood, 
Gahe Aaftab-E-Jahaa’n Bood

Gahe Darr Malak Tuyi Mukhtafi,
Gahe Darr Bashar Toh Ayaa’n Shudi

Ba-Tajalliyat-E-Qidam Tuyi, 
Ba-Tayunat-E-Aa’dam Tuyi

Gahe Jalwahaa-E-Ayaa’n Shudi, 
Gahe Jalwahaa-E-Nihaa’n Shudi

Mera Imtiyaz Bas Aie’n Qadr, 
Tuyi Rehnuma Tuyi Rah-Barr

Mera Faqr Aie’n Ke Tu Ba-Mani, 
Mera Dam-Ba-Dam Tu Amaa’n Shudi

Tu Agar Ba-Qawahi Wisaal-E-Ouu, 
Toh Khudi Bidaa’n Ke Hijab-E-Ouu

Tu Basho Chunay Ke Daree’n Jahaa’n, 
Na Ayaa’n Shudi Na Nihaa’n Shudi

Ba-Tufail-E-Noori Toh Aamira, 
Ba Nabi Rasi Ba-Khuda Rasi

Tu Badaa’n Ke Eknou’n Ba-Faiz-E-Oou, 
Ba Nigah-E-Khaaliq-E-Jaan Shudi
