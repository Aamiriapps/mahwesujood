                       YA RABBI INNI AAJIZU

Ya Rabbi In-ni Aajizun Fajalni Minka Qaadira
In-ni Elayka Tayebunn Fajalni Minka Taahira

In-ni Asaitu Daaemamm Ma Sirrtu Khattu Salehann
Fe Fee-li Laisa Saalihu’n Kuntu Li-ghairi Aamira

Aznabtu In-ni Aamidann Kaifa Akhoolu Sahiyann
Laisa Ka-misili Muznibunn Kulli Raheemann Ghafeera

In-ni Fakheerunn Aajizunn Abdunn Zaleelunn Jahelunn
Antal Azeezul Khadiru Taarifu Minn-E-Maajira

Unnzur Elieyna Ra’himann Anzeel Alaina Rahmatunn
Vaghfir lana Aamalana Am-Batinann Am-Zaahira

Minkal Hayathu Tasbuthu Wal Mauthu Minni Yasbuthu
Maaza Akhoolu Baadahu Ma Kaana Minni Maajira

Antal Aleemu Daemann Wal Ilmu Feeka Saabitunn
Maaza Yakunu Baadahu Wal Jahlu Kaana Sadira

Antal Muridul Khadriu Taf-Alu Kullann Ta-Dilu
Man-Za Yuridu Baa-Daka Wal Abdu Qattu Madara

Antal Khadeerul Ghalibuu Tughlebu Kullan Thakhlukhu
In-ni Elaiyka Aajizunn Fajalni Min-Ka Maahira

Antas Saami-uu Dae-Mann Wal Abdu Ghairu Saami-an
Giy-thu Eleyka Dayeyann Fas-Maa-li Minni Maajara

Antal Baseeru Tubsiru Laa Yabsuru Mann Ghairuka
Unzur Elaa Ahwaalihi Mal-laa Yakunu Naazera

Antal Kaleem-Ul Waaqi-oo Mamm Ma Siwa Kal Abkamu
Sirr Tu Elayka Saamitann Kulli Kareemann Naasira

Kaifa Ou-Addii Haqqahu Mann Allama Ma Aktubu
Ell-Lamm Ajidhuu Haa Huna Ma-Qultu Ilmann Zaahira

Mai Yahya Fee Haa Dae-Mann Mann Kaana Fee-Ha Maeiytunn
In-ni Amutu Daayimal lou Anni Aheya Zaahira

Aalamuhu Mash-hurathun Aadatuhu Masoomatunn
Ustazeeyunn Naumani-Yu Misluhu Saara Naadira

Wal Malumathul Bathinyyathu Tahsulu Minal Lazi
Samm Mahu Rabbu-Hu Nooriyann Summitu Baadu Aamira
